FAQ about our restaurant


Q.: I have particular food restriction, am I able to have a meal?

> A.: For sure! We will be glad to help you and propose dishes that may be suitable for you.

 

Q.: I would like to organise a meal  for a party, how can I do it?

> A.: Please contact us,  telling us all your requirements we will revert with our best offer
Phone +91.901234578 e-mail info@restaurantFoodParadise.com

 

Q.: Which are your specialities

> A.: We have a full range menu from starters to maincourse,from shakes to desserts all fresh and in season.
We strongly suggest our regional plates and speacial menu!

 

Q.: Do you offer non-spicy dishes as well?

> A.: Many Asian dishes call for very hot spices. Especially spicy dishes are marked as such on our menu.
Please let us know if you do not like spicy food when ordering your meal.
We are able to de-spice the dishes according to your preferences.

 

Q.: Should I make a table reservation?

> A.: We strongly advise to book a table although it is not compulsory,
especially on Week-ends which are more busy days.

 

Q.: Do you offer group discounts?

> A.: Rather than offer a discount to one particular group of guests,
we believe our responsibility is to make sure we're providing the best dining value possible to every guest
